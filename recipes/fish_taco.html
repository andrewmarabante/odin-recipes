Fish Tacos:

Description:

Scared of living on the edge and taking chances? Scared of finding out what life is all about? Scared of eating seafood because you might get food poisoning? Then click away from this page and go back to your boring life. Those who answered no to all of the above, find out what were all about.

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Fish of Choice
  • Cilantro
  • Radish
  • Shredded Cheese

How To Make:

  1. Start grilling you fish meat
  2. Chop Cilantro and Radish
  3. Add your tortilla to the grill
  4. Once both the tortilla and meat are done, Assemble your tacos how you want
  5. Eat Up!
