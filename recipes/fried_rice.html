Fried Rice

Description:

Have you ever tasted some crunchy, delicious, out of this world with texture fried rice? Well too bad. This is our recipe for the soggiest, most wet, bland fried rice you've ever had in your life. Check it out below.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Bell Peppers
  • Green Onions
  • Chicken
  • Water (lots of it)

How To Make:

  1. Start grilling chicken
  2. Chop Bell Peppers and Onions
  3. Add rice, peppers, and onions to grill
  4. *Most Important Step: ADD LOTS OF WATER
  5. Soggy Rice Complete
  6. Drink Up!
